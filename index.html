Moto app

As a motorcycle rider myself, I found the need for a place that would gather information for other motorcyclist. In the application you can read interesting blogs about motorcycles, accessories and other relevant content. In the hiking area, you can find a large number of cycling routes that have been recommended and tested by experienced riders and include several points of interest (gas station, lookout for photography, recommended restaurant). In addition to the application there is a market for motorcycles and accessories. The application was written in Kotlin in the MVVM architecture, Dagger-Hilt for DI, use of firestore as a database, ROOM an offline database, Navigation, Auth, View binidng, Data binding, Animations, and the use of a large number of APIs such as Google Maps Google Direction
